Q.9 As per Accounting Standard 24, a discontinuing operation is a component of an enterprise being disposed through a single plan and indicate a separate major line of business and geographical area of an enterprise and distinguished component for financially & operation for reporting purpose.

A component may be disposed substantially (i.e. cut off) or piecemeal or terminating through abandonment

Following are the disclosure & presentation requirement of discontinuing operations
1) Specify the operation of the enterprise which discontinued
2) Specify the date and a period of discontinued need component
3) The carrying amount of assets and liabilities on the date of discontinued operation.
4) Specify Income & Expenses on that date.
