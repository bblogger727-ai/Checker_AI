Q.1 As per AS18 'Related party disclosure', An individual has interest in the voting power that gives them control or significant influence over the enterprises and has financial and operating decision policy decision, then shall be considered as related party.

In the given case, SP. hotels limited enters an agreement with Mr. A for running the day to day management of the hotel, although he does not own any voting power in SP hotels limited.

Conclusion :- If Mr. A entered into agreement for running the day to day management of the hotel, which all financial and operating policy taken by BOD, although he does not have any voting power, and hence, he shall not be considered as related party.
