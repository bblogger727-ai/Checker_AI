Q3. Working Note

Pepsi Co. Ltd
CoCo Cola Ltd
Machine Cola Co. (UK)
BOAR
Indian Co

- Answer
- A non resident has undertaken or proposes to undertake 
  transaction with resident wherein he wishes to enquire on 
  matter related to question of law or facts, he shall make 
  an application to BOAR.
- BOAR provides ruling against transaction entered or proposed 
  to be undertaken.
- However, if the case in relation to similar transaction is pending 
  with ITA, ITAT, HC or SC then the application shall 
  be rejected by BOAR.
- In given case, Machine Cola Co. has made an application 
  to BOAR relating to rate of withhold tax applicable to it & 
  CoCo Cola Ltd. had made similar application to A.O.
- Since the case is already resting with ITA, the rejection 
  stands justifiable
