Proposed Answer

o As per sec 92A, where one enterprise holds more than 26% of voting power directly/indirectly in another enterprise, such enterprise are deemed to be Associate enterprise (A.E.)
o If when 2 enterprises become A.E. of such another enterprise, then such 2 enterprises shall also be A.E.
o Based on above scenario, ND Ltd & TY Inc. are A.E.
o When one A.E. provide guarantee/deposit against loan taken by another A.E. & interest on such loan exceeds 10x then Sec 94B shall apply.
o Max Int allowable on each loan shall be 30% of EBITDA or Actual, whichever is lower. Balance int can be carried forward upto 8 A.Y.
