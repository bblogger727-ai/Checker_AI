Q4. Project Import

- Importer when starting new plant or wants to expand its business, it requires various machinery, instruments & apparatus.
- These goods have are different & has different rate of duty.
- If the person imports the same, he shall be liable to pay heavy custom duty making project inviable.
- In such case Project Import comes into picture wherein goods are imported at concessional rate for the entire project.

Q5. Computation of Assessable Value

Particulars

Cost of Machine (ex-factory)  
(+) Transport charges to port for shipment  
(+) Handling charges for loading machine  
(+) Design charges and engineering work  
Total Free on Board (FOB)  

FOB in ₹  
(+) Freight ($2000 x 70)  
(+) Insurance (1827000 x 1.125%)  
Assessable Value (CIF) [rounded off]

Notes:

- Buying commission do not form part of Assessable Value
- Value up until port of importation are the ones that are considered in determining assessable value therefore
- Unloading & handling charges paid at place of importation & transport charges from Mumbai to Cochin port are ignored
- Since Insurance charges are unascertained, it is computed at 1.125% of FOB.
