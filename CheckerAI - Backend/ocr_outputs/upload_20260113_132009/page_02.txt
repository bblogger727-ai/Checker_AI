**Q2. Anti Dumping Duty**

- Such duty is imposed when goods are dumped at reduced prices causing injury to domestic market.
- Such duty can be imposed retrospectively only when following conditions are satisfied.
  ➤ There is history of such dumping
  ➤ Such dumping has caused major serious injury overtime.

Hence in given scenario Chaintop Industries has to prove the above conditions to make antidumping duty liable on same retrospectively.

**Q3. Computation of Custom Duty Payable**

| Particulars | ₹ |
|-------------|---|
| Assessable Value of Sodium Nitrate | 30,00,000 |
| Basic Custom duty @10% | 3,00,000 |
| Social Welfare Surcharge @10% | 30,000 |
| Safeguard duty @30% | 9,00,000 |
| ➤ Total value incl. Custom duty | 42,30,000 |
| IGST @ 12% | 5,07,600 |
| ➤ Total Custom duty | 12,30,000 |
| ➤ Total custom duty + IGST payable | 17,37,600 |

**Note**

- Safeguard duty is not imposed when goods are imported from developing countries if goods imported from developing countries < 8% of total imports of such goods.
- In given case & since the share of import of Sodium Nitrate against total imports from developing country is 4%, safeguard duty shall be levied.
