Q8. 
- Since the order for home consumption is passed & relevant duty at such time was already paid, therefore importer is not liable for any differential duty.
- Rate of duty even as on date of filing BOE for home consumption or arrival of vessel/aircraft whichever is later shall apply.
- Just because in given case BL Ltd. does not made application to store such goods in warehouse until actual use/sale does not account for non clearance.

Q9. 
- If goods are found defective or not as per specification importer has respective options:—
1) Re-export
   In such case refund of import duty shall be considered from date of re-export.
2) Relinquishment of Title
   Herein refund of import duty shall be from date of relinquishment.
3) Destroy
   Date of destruction shall be considered as date for granting refund of import duty.
