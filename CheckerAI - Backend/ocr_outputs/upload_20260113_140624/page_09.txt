(b) Following person can be register as GST Practitioner.  
i) Any person who is citizen of India, sound mind, not an insolvent not convicted any court.  
ii) Satisfies following conditions -  

1) is a retired officer of commercial tax department of any state.  
2) enrolled as sales Tax Practitioner or tax Return Preparer.  
3) has passed notified examination.  
4) fin passed final exam of ICSI/ ICST/ ICW.
