Q4 (a)

As per CGST 2017, Under section 7 in accordance with Schedule I activities to be treated as supply, even without consideration.

i) Permanent transfer of business assets or disposal where ITC has been availed.

ii) Supply of goods or services or both between related person or distinct person made in furtherance of business. Further, provided that gift given by employer to employee not exceeding 50,000 do not qualify as goods or service.

iii) Supply of goods,
a) By Principal to agent to make supply on behalf of Principal.
b) By agent to Principal where agent undertake supply receive such goods on behalf of Principal.

iv) Importation of services from related person without consideration in furtherance of business from his establishment.
