Ans 2(A) As per Sec. 68 of the Companies Act, 2013. A company can buy-back its own shares out of:

i) Free Reserves
ii) Securities Premium &
iii) Proceeds of Fresh Issue of Shares.

However, Revaluation Reserve shall not be considered for the purpose of Buy-Back.

Circumstances in which a company is prohibited to Buy-Back its own shares:

1) A company cannot buy-back of same class or kind of equity shares previously issued before Buy-Back.
