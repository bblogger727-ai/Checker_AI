the registrar to suo-moto record
the satisfaction of charge against any such
property or undertaking if the charge
holder and the company upon whose
assets charge was created fails to file
the satisfaction of charge.
Registrar do register the charge only
if it gets any intimation from the charge
registrant, that such property or under-
taking is free from charge (either partly
or fully) and or the title deeds are free
from charge or dues have been settled.
In such a case, Registrar may
issue a Show-cause Notice to to the

asking why the charge is not to be
recorded by it.
However, if the request is made by
other person(s) to satisfy the charge and
such request bears sign of the charge
