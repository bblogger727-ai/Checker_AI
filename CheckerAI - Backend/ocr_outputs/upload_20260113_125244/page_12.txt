1. **Schedule-I** - Such transactions are strictly prohibited.

2. **Schedule-II** - & such transactions require prior approval of central government.

3. **Schedule-III** - Transactions which require prior approval of RBI, if such drawal exceeds specified limit of $2,50,000 under Liberalised Remittance Scheme.
