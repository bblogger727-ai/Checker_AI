(i) As per Section 13, the time of supply of services
    is under forward charge is:

  * Date of issue of invoice or date of receipt
    of payment whichever is earlier, if the
    invoice was issued within 30 days from date of
    supply of service

                                    or

  * Date of provision of service or date of receipt
    of payment whichever is earlier, if the
    invoice was not issued within 30 days from
    date of supply of service.

In this case, provisions of Section 13 will not
apply since GPT & Co has not issued an
invoice or entered payment in its books of
accounts. The date of supply of service is also
not available. Therefore time of supply will be
date on which recipient of service shows the
payment in his books of accounts.

Therefore time of supply will be 4th April, date
on which Apartment Owners' Association showed
payment in their books of accounts

(ii) As per Section 12, time of supply of a registered
person making outward supply of goods
is:

* Date of issue of invoice or
* Date on which invoice ought to be
have been issued.

In the case of continious supply of goods,
where successive statements of accounts or
successive payments are made, the time of
supply will be the date of issue of
invoice or for each statement of accounts
or for each such successive payments.
In this case
invoice will be issued on date of
payments of Rs 2 lakhs made on July 5,
August 5 & September 5.

Therefore time of supply will be July 5th,
August 5th & September 5th respectively.

[Question 3(a)]
In case of any omission, or mistake, made in
the eway bill, such eway bill cannot be
edited or modified, once generated. The only
option will be cancellation of such eway bill
within 24 hours of generation of such eway bill
and generation of a new eway bill with
the correct details.
However, such eway bill can be updated if the
mistake was of vehicle number.

Therefore Mr X cannot edit or modify the eway
bill once it has been generated. He will have
to cancel such eway bill within 24 hours of such
generation and issue a new eway bill.

is liable to obtain registration if his aggregate
& turnover exceeds the threshold limit i.e
₹10 lakhs for special category states, being
Manipur, Mizoram, Tripura & Nagaland & ₹20 lakhs
for the rest of the states. for supply of services.
However, services provided by a business
facilitator to a banking company in a taxable
territory & in the rural area branch is exempt.

In this case, Dharun is engaged exclusively in supply
of exempt services.
