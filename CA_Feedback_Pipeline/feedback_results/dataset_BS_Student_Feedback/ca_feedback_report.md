### Conclusion
You had the right building blocks but stumbled at the final assembly — a small conceptual slip in the goodwill computation cost you dearly. Nail the sequence: always arrive at Fair Value of Net Identifiable Assets after DTL before computing Goodwill, and you'll ace this type of question next time!
